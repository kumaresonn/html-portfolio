The Best Movies According to Kums

My Top 3 Movies of all time

Titanic

A timeless epic of love and tragedy, set against the backdrop of one of history's greatest maritime disasters.

Jurassic Park

A groundbreaking adventure that brings dinosaurs to life with thrilling action, awe-inspiring visuals, and a cautionary tale of scientific ambition.

Avatar

A visually stunning masterpiece that transports audiences to an immersive alien world, blending breathtaking visuals with environmental themes and epic storytelling.
